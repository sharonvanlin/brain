💡 Not a replacement. An enabler. ▶
"CIGS flexible panels unlock solar power where solar couldn't go before."
CIGS flexible panels don't replace crystalline silicon for standard rooftops. They enable solar power to go where it couldn't go before — curved, lightweight, and portable surfaces. When portability, weight savings, and conformability are the priority, CIGS is the outstanding choice.
CIGS不是替代晶硅，而是让太阳能去到晶硅去不了的地方。

价格 & 产品异议处理
⚖️ "晶硅更便宜" ▶
You're right — crystalline silicon is cheaper per watt on flat rooftops. But if your surface is curved, weight-sensitive, or architecturally constrained, crystalline simply can't be used. The question isn't which is cheaper — it's which one actually works for your application.
晶硅确实每瓦更便宜。但如果您的表面是曲面、重量敏感或有结构限制，晶硅根本装不上去。问题不是哪个便宜——而是哪个能用。
📉 "效率比晶硅低" ▶
Efficiency ratings are lab numbers. In real conditions — heat, shade, diffuse light — CIGS maintains output where crystalline drops significantly. On a 65°C rooftop, our 100W panel outputs 84.8W vs 82W for mono. And we fit surfaces mono panels can't reach at all.
效率是实验室数据。65°C屋顶，我们100W板实际84.8W，晶硅只有82W。而且我们能装在晶硅装不了的地方。
⏳ "保质期比晶硅短" ▶
⚠️ 买家一定会问，必须准备好
You're right that flexible modules use polymer encapsulation rather than glass — the trade-off is intentional. Where weight and flexibility matter more than a 30-year static installation, CIGS is the right tool. For permanent rooftop installations, rigid panels win. For RV, marine, and portable applications, a 10-year warranty covers the typical use cycle perfectly.
柔性模组用聚合物封装而非玻璃，这是有意为之的取舍。永久屋顶装晶硅赢，RV/船舶/便携场景10年质保完全够用。
🔄 "能反复弯曲吗" ▶
Our modules are designed for conforming to a fixed curved surface, not for dynamic repeated flexing. Once adhered to your RV roof or boat deck, they stay in that shape — no repeated bending stress. This is how they're intended to be used and why they perform reliably.
我们的模组设计用于贴合固定曲面，不是用于反复弯折。粘贴到RV屋顶或船面后保持形状，没有反复弯曲应力，这是正确使用方式。
CIGS 核心参数
🔬 CIGS是什么 ▶
全名：Copper Indium Gallium Selenide（铜铟镓硒）
四种金属元素直接沉积在柔性基底上，天生柔性轻薄，不是硅片切出来的。薄膜层只有1-2微米厚（人类头发的1/100），却能吸收大部分阳光。
厚度1.7mm
重量1.7 kg/m²
弯曲半径min 0.5m
温度系数-0.38%/°C
效率范围14.2–16.9%
工作温度-40°C ~ +85°C
⚡ 温度系数怎么解释 ▶
基准：25°C。温度每升高1°C，功率下降0.38%。
夏天65°C屋顶（高出40°C）
CIGS: 100W × (1-40×0.38%) = 84.8W
晶硅: 100W × (1-40×0.45%) = 82W
天气越热，CIGS优势越明显
